About Us

Download Subject Assessment
View Subject Assessment as a Google Doc

Nana Sweet Tea Shop, an Asian tea brand, is one of the leading and fastest expanding milk tea brands in Australia, with over 80 branches placed in key locations nationwide.

We are dedicated to make world-class products
• Exclusive premium ingredients from Taiwan
• FDA accredited
• Consistent quality
• Exciting seasonal products created by in-house chefs

All tea shops are operated by Emerald Leaf Franchising Corporation or by its franchisees bound by franchise agreements. Nana Sweet operations are strenuously designed to provide consistent and quality service in every branch. Our stringent management system and dynamic team of market analysts, concept designers, and idea generators guarantee that our business flourishes.

While we're busy boba-ing, we've managed to win the Tearific Awards three consecutive years, 2021, 2022 and 2023. We're hoping for even better years to come.
